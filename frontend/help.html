How to change password?
To change your password, go back to the home page, click on your avatar in the top right corner, and select Account. Under the "Membership & Billing" section, click on the blue Change password link and enter your old/new passwords to complete.
Connection Error: Why can't I watch movies?
If you encounter an error where the movie list does not load, please verify if the Backend Server (FastAPI) is running (use the command uvicorn main:app --reload). If the server is on, check your network connection or try pressing F5 to reload the page.
Accepted Payment Methods
SmartFlix currently supports payments via International Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, JCB, Amex), popular regional e-wallets, and direct payments via domestic ATM cards.
